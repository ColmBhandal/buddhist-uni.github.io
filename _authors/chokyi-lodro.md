---
title: "Dzongsar Khyentse Chökyi Lodrö"
---

Dzongsar Khyentse Chökyi Lodrö (1893–1959) was a major proponent of the Rimé movement and was a teacher of many of the major figures in 20th-century Tibetan Buddhism.
